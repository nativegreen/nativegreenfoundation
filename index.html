Few Words About Us

Native Green Foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation based out of Guwahati, Assam, India. The organisation is driven by the vision of creating a greener, cleaner, and healthier environment.

Social Forestry and Tree Plantation Conservation of Natural Resources Renewable Sources of Energy Waste Management Pollution Control Disaster Relief Awareness Programs on afforestation, waste management, conservation of natural resources, pollution, etc.

Our Objective

A Non-Profit Organisation Driven by the Vision of creating a Greener, Cleaner, and Healthier Environment.

  • To conduct tree plantation activities.
  • To conduct activities leading to cleaner and hygienic environment.
  • To conduct programs on ‘Waste Management’ activities and ‘Recycling/Reuse’ techniques.
  • To conduct activities or research to find solutions to environmental pollution problems.
  • To plan, organise and implement programs and projects for energy conservation and emphasis on the usage of renewable sources of energy.
  • To plan, organise and implement programs and projects for natural resources (such as water and fuel) management & conservation.
  • To organise awareness programs on environmental issues, natural resource management and conservation, renewable resources, plantation, etc.
  • To facilitate relief efforts in case of natural or man-made disasters.
