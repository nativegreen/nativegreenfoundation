Photography Competition - Urban Ecology in Guwahati

22nd December, 2022

The Online Photography Competition was organized from 22nd December 2022 to 15th January 2023 to spread awareness on urban ecology in Guwahati, Assam. The event was also supported by Bhaskar’s pinkVilla, a Guwahati-based salon and Moukhap, a Guwahati-based documentary production house. Bhaskar’s pinkVilla provided gift coupons for the winners, and Moukhap extended their helping hands to assess the photographs of the participants and event promotional activities. The promotion of the event was done via social media such as Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp and the entries were received via Google Forms. The winners were announced on 25th January 2023 on the social media platforms of the Society.
