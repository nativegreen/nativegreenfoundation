International Forest Day Online Art Contest

21st March, 2022

To observe International Forest Day 2022, an Online Art Competition (Theme – Forest and Environment) was organized from 11th to 19th March 2022 to spread awareness on forest and environment. The promotion of the event was done via social media such as Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn and the entries were received via email. The online campaign had reached more than 1000 individuals online and more than 40 entries from various parts of India were received. The entries were divided into three groups i.e., Group A (age: 4 years to 10 years), Group B (age: 11 years to 17 years), and Group C (age: 18 years and above).

The winners were announced on 21st March 2022, i.e. on International Forest Day 2022 on the social media platforms. All the entries were published on Instagram. The best entries were awarded with medals, certificates, and goodies which were shared with the winners via courier service. All the participants were awarded with E-Certificates of Participation. Special Thanks to Alight Hair and Beauty Salon, Bhetapara, Guwahati – 781028, Assam and the Honourable Patrons of Native Green Foundation for sponsoring the event.
