Plantation at Nemcare Group of Institutions, Mirza

On June 10, 2023, the Native Green Foundation carried out a tree plantation program at the NEMCARE Group of Institutions in Mirza, Assam, India. We planted 20 numbers of plant saplings, comprising 10 "Neem" (Azadirachta indica) saplings and 10 "Krishnashura" (Delonix regia) saplings, within the premises of the NEMCARE Group of Institutions.


We extend our heartiest thanks to all the members of our NGO and the staff of the NEMCARE Group of Institutions for their cooperation and extending their help to make the event successful. We especially thank Dr. Apurba Talukdar, Deputy Registrar of Academics, NEMCARE Group of Institutions, for providing the utmost support.
